# Centripetal Force $\vec{F_{c}}$
---
$$\begin{align}
&\vec{F_{net}}=m\vec{a}\\
&\therefore \vec{F_{c}}=m\vec{a_{c}}\\
&\vec{F_{c}}= \frac{mv^{2}}{r} = \frac{n4\pi^{2}r}{T^{2}}
\end{align}$$
