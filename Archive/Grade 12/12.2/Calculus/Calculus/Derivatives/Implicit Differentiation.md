# Implicit Differentiation
---
 > When given equations like in a circle, you can take the derivative of **both sides**
 > $$\begin{align}
x^{2}+y^{2}&=25\\
\frac{d(x^{2}+y^{2}}{dx}&=0\\
\frac{dx^{2}}{dx}+ \frac{dy^{2}}{dx}&=0\\
2x+2y\cdot\dot{y}&=0\\
\dot{y}&=\frac{-x}{y}
\end{align}$$
