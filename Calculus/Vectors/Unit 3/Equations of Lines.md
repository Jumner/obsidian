# Equations of Lines
---
## In $\mathbb{R}^{2}$ space
### Vector Equations
$\vec{m}$ = (run, rise)
In other words, it is the vector that points from **y intercept** to **x intercept**


$$\begin{align}
&(x,y)=(x_{o},y_{o})+t(a,b)\\
&t\in\mathbb{R}
\end{align}$$
### Parametric Equations
$$\begin{align}
&x=x_{o}+at\\
&y=y_{o}+bt\\
&t\in\mathbb{R}
\end{align}$$

### Finding the Equation
#### Location
the **y intercept**
#### Direction
the **slope**
## Direction [[Vectors|Vector]]
Any [[Vectors|Vector]] that is parallel to a line.
