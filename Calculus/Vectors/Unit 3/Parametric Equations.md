# Parametric Equations
---
## In $\mathbb{R}^{2}$ space
$$\begin{align}
&x=x_{o}+at\\
&y=y_{o}+bt\\
&t\in\mathbb{R}
\end{align}$$
## In $\mathbb{R}^{3}$ Space
$$\begin{align}
&x=x_{o}+at\\
&y=y_{o}+bt\\
&z=z_{o}+ct\\
&t\in\mathbb{R}
\end{align}$$
## Plane In $\mathbb{R}^{3}$ Space
$$\begin{align}
&x=x_{0}+s\vec{a_{x}}+t\vec{b_{x}}\\
&y=y_{0}+s\vec{a_{y}}+t\vec{b_{y}}\\
&z=z_{0}+s\vec{a_{z}}+t\vec{b_{z}}\\
&s,t \in \mathbb{R}
\end{align}$$
