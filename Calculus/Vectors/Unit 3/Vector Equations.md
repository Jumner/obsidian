# Vector Equations
---
## In $\mathbb{R}^{2}$ Space
$$\begin{align}
&\vec{r} = \vec{r}_{o}+ t\vec{m}, t\in\mathbb{R}\\
&(x,y)=(x_{o},y_{o})+t(a,b)\\
\end{align}$$
## In $\mathbb{R}^{3}$ Space
$$\begin{align}
&\vec{r} = \vec{r}_{o}+ t\vec{m}, t\in\mathbb{R}\\
&(x,y,z) = (x_{o},y_{o},z_{o}) + t(a,b,c)
\end{align}$$
## Plane In $\mathbb{R}^3$ Space
$$\vec{r} = (x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}) + s\vec{a} + t\vec{b}, s,t \in \mathbb{R}$$
